## About Me (˶˃ ᵕ ˂˶) .ᐟ.ᐟ

I’m a software engineer who genuinely loves building things that probably started as the most random idea possible.

I currently work in the cybersecurity field at <a href="https://www.eset.com/" target="_blank">@ESET</a>, focusing on the technical side of implementing and protecting technology at scale. My background in software engineering and my interest in security come from the same belief: creation matters, and what we create deserves to be protected. Innovation only continues when the systems, ideas, and people behind it are secure enough to keep moving forward.

Outside of work, I spend most of my time making side projects, experimenting with AI/ML, designing interfaces, building web & mobile apps, exploring branding systems, and honestly just creating things for fun because I love the process of bringing ideas to life. Software has always felt a little magical to me, like turning thoughts into something real that people can actually interact with.

I also really enjoy building things with friends, whether it’s serious products, chaotic experiments, or concepts that only exist because someone said “wait… this would actually be cool.”

Most people online know me as **"iCY"** — originally short for _“I am Cynthia,”_ which started as one of my first usernames and somehow never left. Now it’s basically part of my identity across projects, experiments, and everything creative I touch. So yeah, you can call me iCY if you want :)
